# Uncertainty Reporting

- report confidence levels for key conclusions (high, medium, low)
- separate verified facts from reasonable inference from speculation
- call out what is unknown and what cannot be verified with available sources
- do not present inference as fact
- when multiple interpretations exist, present the strongest two or three with evidence
- note when findings depend on assumptions that could change
