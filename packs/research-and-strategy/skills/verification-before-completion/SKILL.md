# Verification Before Completion

Use this skill before calling research work done.

## Goal-backward verification

A report that exists is not a report that answers the research question. Verify backward:

1. What must be TRUE for the research question to be answered?
2. What evidence must EXIST for those answers to be supported?
3. Are the evidence grades, confidence levels, and citations complete?

## Verification steps

- confirm the original research question is answered, not a nearby question
- verify all claims have evidence links or explicit uncertainty labels
- check that contradictory evidence is reported, not hidden
- check that the executive summary matches the detailed findings
- note any intentional scope reduction from the original question
