# Research and Strategy Pack Quickstart

## Profiles

| Profile | Use when | MCPs | Risk level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| desk | reading, local synthesis, note-taking | 5 local | low |
| analyst (default) | cross-repo investigation, standards comparison | 7 local | low-medium |
| investigation | market research, competitive analysis, broad evidence gathering | 10 (includes web) | medium-high |

## Agents

| Agent | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| trend-researcher | Scan sources for emerging patterns and market signals |
| competitive-analyst | Compare alternatives across structured dimensions |
| evidence-reviewer | Grade source quality and flag evidence gaps |
| report-writer | Transform findings into structured, citation-backed reports |
| executive-summary-writer | Compress reports into decision-ready summaries |

## Key skills

- `context-budget` -- keep research sessions focused and traceable
- `writing-plans` -- plan research before executing it
- `verification-before-completion` -- verify findings answer the question asked

## Rules

All research output follows four rules:
- evidence-over-claims: every assertion needs a source
- uncertainty-reporting: confidence levels on key conclusions
- citation-discipline: primary sources preferred, stale sources flagged
- report-structure: executive summary first, findings numbered, recommendations reference findings

## Model guidance

- trend-researcher and competitive-analyst: standard model (breadth over depth)
- evidence-reviewer: strongest model (skeptical judgment matters)
- report-writer and executive-summary-writer: strongest model (precision in synthesis)
