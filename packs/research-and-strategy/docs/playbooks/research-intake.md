# Research Intake

Before starting any research initiative, define:

1. **Research question**: one clear question that the output must answer.
2. **Scope**: what is in scope, what is explicitly out of scope.
3. **Source types**: primary data, vendor docs, analyst reports, community signals.
4. **Output format**: trend brief, comparison matrix, full report, executive summary.
5. **Quality bar**: what confidence level is needed for the decision at hand.
6. **Deadline**: when the output is needed and what can be cut if time is short.

## Intake checklist

- [ ] Research question is specific and answerable
- [ ] Scope boundaries are explicit
- [ ] Source types are identified
- [ ] Output format is agreed
- [ ] Quality bar is defined (discovery-grade vs. decision-grade)
- [ ] Time constraint is known
