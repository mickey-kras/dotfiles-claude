# Campaign Build

## Research first

Before drafting, establish:
- target audience and their primary problem or interest
- competitive landscape: what already exists on this topic
- search intent and keyword opportunities if the content is discoverable
- existing brand assets and voice guidelines to reuse

## Build sequence

1. Anchor the campaign in one message and one audience.
2. Define content pillars and map them to the reader journey stage.
3. Create a hero narrative and two or three supporting content assets.
4. Define the visual system before generating or requesting assets.
5. Track claim support and image provenance as part of the draft, not after it.
6. Produce channel adaptations last.

## Quality gates

- strategy review before drafting (content-strategist -> editorial-reviewer)
- claims and citations verified before channel adaptation
- brand consistency checked by brand-guardian before publishing
- channel adaptations reviewed for platform-specific quality

## Repurposing

Identify repurposing paths early:
- long-form article -> social media thread -> email summary
- video script -> blog post -> short clips
- research brief -> executive summary -> social proof quotes
