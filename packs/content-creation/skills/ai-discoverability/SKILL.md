# AI Discoverability

Use this skill when optimizing content for AI-powered search and recommendation
engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini) alongside traditional SEO.

## When to use

- Before publishing long-form content that should appear in AI citations
- When auditing existing content for AI engine visibility
- When planning content strategy around AI-driven discovery channels

## Checklist

- Does the content answer a clear, specific question that AI engines are likely to surface?
- Are key claims structured as direct, citable statements rather than buried in narrative?
- Does the content include structured data (schema markup, FAQ blocks, definition lists)?
- Are primary sources cited inline so AI engines can verify and attribute?
- Does the page have a clear, descriptive title and meta description?
- Is the content structured with semantic headings that map to search intent?
- Are there competitor pages that AI engines currently cite instead? What do they do differently?

## Deliverable

After running this checklist, produce:
- a list of structural improvements for AI citation likelihood
- recommended schema markup additions
- content gaps relative to what AI engines currently surface for the target queries
- priority ranking: quick wins vs long-term investments
